enum CppTrader::ITCH::SystemEventCodes
strong

System event codes.

Enumerator
START_OF_MESSAGES 

Start of Messages. Outside of time stamp messages, the start of day message is the first message sent in any trading day.

START_OF_SYSTEM_HOURS 

Start of System hours. This message indicates that NASDAQ is open and ready to start accepting orders.

START_OF_MARKET_HOURS 

Start of Market hours. This message is intended to indicate that Market Hours orders are available for execution.

END_OF_SYSTEM_HOURS 

End of System hours. It indicates that NASDAQ is now closed and will not accept any new orders today. It is still possible to receive Broken Trade messages and Order Delete messages after the End of Day.

END_OF_MESSAGES 

End of Messages. This is always the last message sent in any trading day.

EMERGENCY_MARKET_CONDITION_HALT 

Emergency Market Condition - Halt: This message is sent to inform NASDAQ market participants that the EMC is in effect. No trading is allowed during the EMC.

EMERGENCY_MARKET_CONDITION_QUOTE_ONLY_PERIOD 

Emergency Market Condition – Quote Only Period: This message is sent to inform NASDAQ market participants that the EMC quotation only period is in effect.

EMERGENCY_MARKET_CONDITION_RESUMPTION 

Emergency Market Condition – Resumption: This message is sent to inform NASDAQ market participants that EMC is no longer in effect.
